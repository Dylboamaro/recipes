Steak Frites

Description

Steak Frites is a simple dish consisting of steak (usually pan seared) and french fries.

Ingredients

  • 1 steak
  • 1 potato
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/4 cup pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Season the steak with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the steak in the skillet.
  4. Cook the steak to your desired doneness.
  5. Remove the steak from the skillet and let it rest.
  6. Peel and cut the potato into fries.
  7. Heat the olive oil in a skillet.
  8. Fry the fries until golden brown.
  9. Remove the fries from the skillet and drain on paper towels.
  10. Serve the steak and fries together.
