Ramen

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat- or fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and uses toppings such as sliced pork

Ingredients

  • 1 package of ramen noodles
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of green onions
  • 1/4 cup of sliced pork
  • 1/4 cup of bean sprouts

Steps

  1. Boil the ramen noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Boil the egg for 7 minutes, then peel and slice it in half.
  3. Chop the green onions.
  4. Boil the sliced pork in water for 5 minutes.
  5. Place the cooked noodles in a bowl.
  6. Top the noodles with the egg, green onions, sliced pork, and bean sprouts.
  7. Pour the broth over the noodles and toppings.
