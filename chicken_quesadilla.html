Chicken Quesadilla

Description

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish and type of taco, consisting of a tortilla that is filled primarily with cheese, and sometimes meats, beans, and spices, and then cooked on a griddle. Traditionally, a quesadilla is made with a corn tortilla, but it can also be made with a flour tortilla, particula rly in northern Mexico and the United States.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 8-inch flour tortillas

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place one tortilla in the skillet.
  3. Sprinkle the chicken and cheese over the tortilla.
  4. Place the second tortilla on top of the chicken and cheese.
  5. Cook until the bottom tortilla is golden brown.
  6. Flip the quesadilla and cook until the other side is golden brown.
  7. Remove from the skillet and cut into wedges.
  8. Serve with salsa and sour cream.
